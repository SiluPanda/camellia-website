Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026 · Last updated: March 15, 2026

Camellia ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Camellia mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We do not receive or store your Google password.
  • Health profile: You may voluntarily provide health conditions (e.g., diabetes, hypertension), allergies (e.g., peanuts, gluten), and dietary preferences. This information is stored on your device and, if you opt in to cloud sync, in our secure cloud database.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Scan data: When you scan a food barcode, we record the barcode number, product name, scan timestamp, and the AI-generated health analysis. This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to the cloud.
  • Usage analytics: We collect anonymized usage data through Firebase Analytics, including screen views, feature usage, and general app interaction patterns. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Crash reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and diagnostic data to improve app stability. Crash reports may include device model, OS version, and stack traces. They do not contain your health data.
  • Performance data: We use Firebase Performance Monitoring to measure app load times and network request latencies. This data is aggregated and anonymized.

1.3 Camera Access

The App requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning food barcodes. Camera images are processed on-device in real-time to detect barcodes. We do not capture, store, or transmit camera images or video.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the core service: Analyze food products against your health profile and generate personalized health assessments and alternative product recommendations.
  • Sync your data: If you sign in, sync your scan history, favorites, and health profile across your devices via Google Cloud Firestore.
  • Improve the App: Use anonymized analytics and crash reports to identify bugs, improve performance, and develop new features.
  • Communicate with you: Respond to support requests or inquiries you send to us.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

Recipient Data Shared Purpose
Google (Firebase) Account info, synced data, analytics, crash reports Authentication, cloud storage, analytics, crash reporting
FatSecret API Barcode numbers Retrieving product nutrition data
AI Model Provider (DeepInfra) Product nutrition data, your health conditions and allergies (anonymized, not linked to your identity) Generating personalized health analysis

When your health conditions and allergies are sent to the AI model for analysis, they are transmitted as anonymized text parameters—not linked to your name, email, or any account identifier. The AI provider does not have access to your identity.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage: Your health profile, scan history, and favorites are stored on your device using encrypted SharedPreferences.
  • Cloud storage: If you sign in, data is synced to Google Cloud Firestore, which encrypts data at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.2+.
  • Backend API: Our backend API runs on Fly.io with HTTPS enforced. API requests are authenticated using Firebase ID tokens. We do not store your health data on our backend servers—data flows through for AI analysis and is not persisted.
  • Access controls: Cloud data is scoped to your authenticated user ID. Other users cannot access your data.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the App.
  • Cloud data: Retained while your account is active. You can delete your cloud data at any time by signing out, which clears synced data from the App. To request complete deletion of cloud data, contact us at support@camellia.app.
  • Analytics and crash data: Retained by Firebase for up to 14 months, then automatically deleted.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal data via your health profile in the App.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of analytics: You can disable Firebase Analytics by adjusting your device's ad tracking settings.
  • Withdraw consent: You can stop using the App and revoke Google Sign-In access through your Google Account settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete that information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States and Singapore (where our backend API is hosted). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:

  • Email: support@camellia.app
